About Us

My name is Ibukun, and I am the proud founder of Bobble Cleaning Service, a house cleaning company dedicated to delivering exceptional services to our clients. As a woman entrepreneur, I started this business with the vision of creating a company that not only provides sparkling clean homes but also places customer satisfaction at the forefront. At Bobble Cleaning Service, we understand the importance of a clean and organized home and the positive impact it has on your daily life. That is why we pay close attention to detail and are committed to offering personalized cleaning services tailored to your specific needs. Our team consists of highly trained and experienced professionals who are passionate about what they do. They are dedicated to making your home look and feel its best. We use eco-friendly products and cleaning methods that are safe for your family and pets while still delivering outstanding results. As a woman-owned business, we are committed to empowering other women and supporting our local community. We believe in equal opportunities and strive to create a diverse and inclusive workplace that reflects the world we live in. Thank you for choosing Bobble Cleaning Service. We truly appreciate your trust and look forward to making your home shine.
